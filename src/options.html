Bot Patterns

Provide one JavaScript regular expression per line (without the surrounding slashes). These patterns are matched against the co-author name, email, and local-part of the email to identify bots.

Defaults include: \[bot\] and (?:^|[+\-._])bot(?:$|[+\-._])

Examples

  • \[bot\] — matches dependabot[bot], github-actions[bot]
  • (?:^|[+\-._])bot(?:$|[+\-._]) — matches snyk-bot, renovate-bot
  • ^codecov(?:-bot)?$ — match local-part exactly codecov or codecov-bot
